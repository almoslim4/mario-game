# Pipebound Panic

An original browser platformer inspired by classic jump-and-stomp games. Open `index.html` in a browser and play.

## Controls

- Move: `A/D` or arrow keys
- Jump: `W`, `Up`, or `Space`
- Dash: `Shift` or `K` (after the dash pepper)
- Fireball: `F` or `J` (after the fire flower)
- Pause: `P`
- Restart current world: `R`
- Side buttons: sound, fullscreen, Assist mode, Zen mode

## Goal

Six worlds stand between Pip and the Core Dragon. Collect coins, sky gems, fire flowers, shields, magnets, and stars. Chain stomps, fireballs, bounce pads and dash rings to crank the Flow multiplier. Earn achievements, set best times, and unlock every world from the level select menu.

## Power-ups

- **Dash pepper** – unlocks a forward dash
- **Cloud feather** – unlocks double jump
- **Fire flower** – throw fireballs that destroy enemies
- **Bubble shield** – absorbs one hit
- **Magnet charm** – pulls coins/gems toward Pip
- **Star power** – brief invincibility and crush damage
- **Heart** – refills one heart; every 100 coins grants a 1-UP

## Bosses and Enemies

New turret enemies lock on and fire bolts — stomp them, dash them, or scorch them with fireballs. Clear Volcano Core to unlock the final showdown: the **Core Dragon**, with three attack phases (leap slam, fire breath, shockwave).

## Prize Blocks

Jump into a `?` block from below. Support blocks beneath a `?` also pop the prize when bumped.

## Play Options

Assist mode starts with more hearts, softer hits, and fall saves. Zen mode reduces particles and shake. Progress, best times, achievements, and lifetime coins are saved locally.
